## Composition

* [PICO DAW](https://www.lexaloffle.com/bbs/?tid=147610), by [@bikibird](https://www.patreon.com/bikibird)

    A Digital Audio Workstation for creating Pico-8 music.

## MIDI Tools

* [Denote](https://bikibird.itch.io/denote), by [@bikibird](https://www.patreon.com/bikibird)

    A tool to convert MIDI data to Pico-8 sfx.

* [midi2pico](https://github.com/BlueAmulet/midi2pico), by [@BlueAmulet](https://ko-fi.com/BlueAmulet)

    A MIDI to PICO-8 converter.

* [midi-to-pico8](https://github.com/andmatand/midi-to-pico8), by [@kittenm4ster](https://kittenm4ster.itch.io/)

    "It just works, sometimes!"

* [midi2pico8dx](https://github.com/MarechalBanane/midi2pico8dx), by [@marechalbanane](https://marechalbanane.itch.io/)

    A small utility that maps MIDI input to keyboard keys for use in PICO-8  .

## Music Libraries

    A library of Pico-8 music released under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license.

* [Defy](https://www.lexaloffle.com/bbs/?pid=128330), by [@bikibird](https://www.patreon.com/bikibird)

## SFX Generators

* [sfxp](https://luchak.itch.io/sfxp), by [@luchak](https://luchak.itch.io/)

    Create sfx fast!

* [Tiny Drops](https://luchak.itch.io/tiny-drops), by [@luchak](https://luchak.itch.io/)

    Bounce tiny drops that fall from the sky to make sfx and music.

## SFX Libraries

* [midilib](https://www.lexaloffle.com/bbs/?tid=49265), by [@packbat](https://packbat.itch.io/) and Pico-8 community

    A Pico-8 sfx implementation of the general MIDI library

## Synthesizers

* [RP-8](https://luchak.itch.io/rp8), by [@luchak](https://luchak.itch.io/)

    Synthesizer and groovebox inspired, by Propellerhead Software's ReBirth RB-338.

* [picoSYNTH](https://johanpeitz.itch.io/picosynth), by [@johanpeitz](https://johanpeitz.itch.io/)

    A playful way to create simple loops and beats.

* [Waporware Modular](https://waporwave.itch.io/wwmodular), by [@waporwave](https://waporwave.itch.io/)

    A modular synth toy.

## Wavetable Tools

* [Wave Designer](https://www.lexaloffle.com/bbs/?tid=140438), by [@luchak](https://luchak.itch.io/)

    A tool to allow both time-domain and frequency-domain editing of the custom waveforms.
